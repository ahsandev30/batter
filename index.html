Our Story

More Than Just a Sports Bar

Founded in 2010 by former MLB player Jake Thompson, Batter Up combines his two passions: baseball and great food. What started as a small neighborhood pub has grown into Austin's premier sports dining destination.

With 32 HD screens, including a 200-inch projector, you won't miss a moment of the action. Our menu features elevated pub classics with a gourmet twist, all made from scratch using local ingredients.

Whether you're here for the big game or just a casual dinner with friends, our friendly staff and lively atmosphere will make you feel like part of the team.
